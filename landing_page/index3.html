Uniconnect Overview

Uniconnect is designed to bring students, lecturers, and administrators together on a single platform. Manage your academic life efficiently, stay connected, and focus on what really matters—learning and success. Whether you're a student navigating your academic journey, a lecturer streamlining your teaching tasks, or an administrator overseeing institutional operations, Uniconnect has everything you need.

Who Are You?

Students

As a student, Uniconnect helps you manage your academic life. View your schedule, deadlines, and announcements. Communicate with lecturers and classmates, access resources, and track your grades and CGPA—all in one place.
